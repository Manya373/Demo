What is Jugaad?

A simple way to turn everyday skills into income and to find trusted local help when you need it.

Jugaad is a community platform where people of all ages can register either as Helpers to offer their time and skills, or as Hirers to request help for tasks like teaching, projects, cooking for small gatherings, or child care.

Not everyone has a formal certification, but many people still know how to do the work well. Jugaad gives them a chance to show what they can do and earn fairly, while nearby families and individuals get affordable and reliable help.

Three age groups. One community.

Clear groups make it easier to match the right work with the right person, while keeping things safe and transparent.

Children & Teens

14 – 18 years

Adults

Retired & Seniors

60+ years

How Jugaad works

A simple four-step flow so that anyone who thinks “I can do it” can actually get started.

1
Create your account

Enter your name, age, phone number, and email so we can verify you and place you in the right age group.

2
Choose your role

Decide whether you want to work as a Helper doing tasks, or act as a Hirer who posts tasks and finds the right people.

3
Post or pick tasks

Helpers browse open tasks nearby, and Hirers clearly describe what work is needed, where, and by when.

4
Connect and complete

Coordinate safely through the platform, complete the work, and build a trusted network inside your own city or town.
